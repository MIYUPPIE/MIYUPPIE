## 👋 About Me

I build intelligent systems across the full stack, from the silicon up. My work sits at the intersection of **AI/ML**, **robotics & mechatronics**, and **production software** — designing the model, wiring the hardware, and shipping the app that ties it together.

- 🧠 **AI & Robotics Engineer** — local-first ML pipelines, computer vision, LLM-driven automation, embedded control.
- 💻 **Full-Stack Developer** — Django / Node back ends, React & Next.js front ends, Flutter mobile.
- ⚙️ **Mechatronics Engineer** — bridging firmware, sensors, and software into real-world products.
- 🏢 **Founder @ [Okhub Technology](https://www.okhubtech.com)** — building products for African founders and communities.
- 📍 Based in **Ibadan, Nigeria** · open to collaboration on AI, robotics, and impact-driven software.
